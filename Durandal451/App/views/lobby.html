Things to know about joining a lobby:

The table on the left displays all the currently available lobbys, which were created by your co-workers.

If you're new to the website, there are a few things you need to know about joining a lobby:

  • Once you pressed the "+" next to the details of the given lobby, you and others will see that you've joined.
  • If the number of players in the lobby reaches the required ammount, those who are in the lobby receive a notification that their game has started.
  • If you can't stay or want to leave the lobby, you can do it by pressing the "-" at then end of the table.
